# Power supply options

The Radxa E20C supports a variety of power supplies with a fixed voltage of 5V input:

- Radxa PD30W

- Apple Adapter 20W

- Anker Adapter 65W

- Xiaomi Adapter 65W

- Greenlink Adapter 65W

- Bessie Adapter 65W

:::tip
Recommended Official 30W Adapter [Radxa PD 30W Adapter](/accessories/pd_30w)
:::
